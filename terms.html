Description of Service

Listena is an English language learning application that provides:

  • Podcasts — AI-generated audio content for English listening practice, with chapter-based navigation and background playback

  • Speaking Practice — Real-time voice conversations with an AI tutor for pronunciation and fluency practice
  • Translation — In-app translation assistance for vocabulary and sentence comprehension
